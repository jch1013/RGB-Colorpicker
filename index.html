RGB Color Picker

How to use this site:

Step 1: select a starter color by clicking one of the twelve initial colored boxes. You should select the color that most closely resembles the color you are looking for.

Step 2: refine the shade of your chosen starter color by clicking boxes below until you find the color you want.

Step 3: copy the rgb color code for your chosen color by right clicking on the color.
